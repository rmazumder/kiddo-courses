📚 Key Words — Learn These!
🔌 1/0
Logic Gate
A tiny circuit that takes one or two inputs (1 or 0) and produces one output based on a rule. The decision-maker inside every computer!
⏫ H/L
HIGH and LOW
HIGH (1) = voltage ON, close to 5V. LOW (0) = voltage OFF, close to 0V. Digital circuits have nothing in between!
📋 TT
Truth Table
A chart showing every possible combination of inputs and what the output will be. One row per combination!
🔄 IC
74xx IC Chip
A chip that contains multiple logic gates in one package. Pin 14 = power (+5V), Pin 7 = ground. Use 5V power!

💡 My Activity Today — Build All 3 Gates!
AND Gate (7408) — Build Steps: (+)rail ──[BTN A]──> Pin 1 ──[10k to GND] (+)rail ──[BTN B]──> Pin 2 ──[10k to GND] Pin 3 (OUT) ──> [330Ω] ──> LED(+) ──> LED(-) ──> GND Pin 14 to (+)rail | Pin 7 to GND NOT Gate (7404) — The Backwards LED! (+)rail ──[BTN]──> Pin 1 ──[10k to GND] Pin 2 (OUT) ──> [330Ω] ──> LED(+) ──> GND Button NOT pressed (0) → LED is ON! (output = 1) Button pressed (1) → LED is OFF! (output = 0)
OR Gate (7432): Identical wiring to AND gate — just swap the chip! Press EITHER button to light the LED.

🔌 4 Experiments You Did Today!
A
AND Gate Challenge
Press only one button on the AND gate circuit.
LED stays OFF — you need BOTH keys to open the treasure chest!
B
OR Gate Door Test
Press EITHER button on the OR gate circuit.
LED lights up with just ONE button — either door lets you in!
C
NOT Gate Backwards!
Build the NOT gate and observe the LED with no button pressed.
LED is ON when button is up — pressing turns it OFF. Everything is reversed!
D
Wand Voltage Hunt
Measure output pins with the multimeter for HIGH and LOW states.
HIGH reads near 5V, LOW reads near 0V — now you know what 1 and 0 really look like!

🔧 Troubleshooting Quick Guide
ProblemQuick Fix
LED always ONCheck 10k pull-down resistors — inputs may be floating HIGH
LED never ONVerify Pin 14 (+5V) and Pin 7 (GND) are connected
Random outputTie unused inputs to GND or VCC — never leave them floating
IC gets hotPower/GND pins reversed — check and fix immediately!
Wand reads middle voltageInput is floating — add a 10k pull-down resistor to GND
