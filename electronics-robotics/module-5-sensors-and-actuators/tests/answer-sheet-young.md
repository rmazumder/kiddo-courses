---

## Teacher Notes & Extensions

**If a student struggles with:**

- **Sensor vs Actuator:** "Sensor = reads (like your eyes). Actuator = does (like your arm)."
- **Servo vs DC Motor:** Show them a real servo moving to specific angles, then a DC motor spinning freely.
- **Distance measurement:** "Ultrasonic is like a bat's echolocation — sound goes out, bounces back, and we measure the time."

**Fun discussion (not graded):**
- "What sensors would you combine to make a smart robot?"
- "Could you use two PIR sensors to detect which direction someone is moving?"
