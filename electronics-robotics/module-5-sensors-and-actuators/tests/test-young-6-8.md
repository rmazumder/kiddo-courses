## Section 1: Multiple Choice (5 questions)

**1. A sensor is used to:**
- A) Spin a wheel
- B) Read the world around it (temperature, light, movement) and send signals to the Arduino
- C) Store electricity
- D) Make sound

**2. The DHT11 sensor measures:**
- A) Only temperature
- B) Only humidity
- C) Temperature AND humidity
- D) Brightness

**3. An ultrasonic sensor works like:**
- A) A camera that takes pictures
- B) A bat using echolocation to find distance
- C) A thermometer
- D) A light bulb

**4. A servo motor is different from a DC motor because:**
- A) A servo moves to exact angles; a DC motor spins continuously
- B) A servo is faster
- C) A DC motor is stronger
- D) They are the same thing

**5. An actuator's job is to:**
- A) Read information from sensors
- B) Display text on a screen
- C) DO something physical — spin, move, or light up
- D) Store data on the computer
