## Section 1: Multiple Choice (5 questions)

**1. A robot needs these basic parts:**
- A) Just a brain (Arduino)
- B) Chassis, motors, brain (Arduino), and sensors
- C) Only wheels
- D) A computer and wifi

**2. Motors need their own battery because:**
- A) They are scared of the Arduino
- B) Motors draw too much current; Arduino pins cannot power them
- C) It looks cool
- D) They do not; they share the Arduino's battery

**3. The L298N motor driver does what?**
- A) Stores electricity
- B) Slows down the motors
- C) Takes small signals from Arduino and sends BIG power to motors
- D) Makes the robot move faster

**4. A line-following robot stays on a black line using:**
- A) GPS
- B) Infrared sensors (like TCRT5000) that see the black line
- C) Cameras
- D) The robot driver guiding it by hand

**5. Bluetooth (HC-05) lets you:**
- A) Make phone calls from the robot
- B) Connect the robot to the internet
- C) Control the robot from your phone wirelessly
- D) Make the robot speak English
