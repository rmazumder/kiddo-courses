## Teacher Notes & Extensions

**If a student struggles with:**

- **Robot anatomy:** "Every robot has a brain (Arduino), muscles (motors), eyes (sensors), and skeleton (chassis)."
- **Motor power:** "Motors need WAY more current than Arduino can give. That is why we need the motor driver — it is like a powerful helper!"
- **Sensors:** "The robot is blind without sensors. Sensors are the robot's eyes, ears, and skin."

**Fun discussion (not graded):**
- "What sensors would your robot need? Why?"
- "Can a robot work without sensors? Why not?"
- "How would you program a robot to find its way out of a maze?"
