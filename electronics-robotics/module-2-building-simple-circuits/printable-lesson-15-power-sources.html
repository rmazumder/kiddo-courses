📚 Key Words — Learn These!
🔋 V
Voltage (V)
How hard the battery "pushes" electricity. A 9V battery pushes 6x harder than a 1.5V AA battery!
💦 mAh
Capacity (mAh)
How long a battery can keep pushing before it runs out. Like a fuel tank — bigger mAh = longer lasting!
📈 +
Series
Batteries wired end-to-end (+ to −). Voltages add up! 3 AA in series = 1.5 + 1.5 + 1.5 = 4.5V
🔄 ∥
Parallel
Batteries wired side by side (+ to +, − to −). Voltage stays the same, but the battery lasts much longer!
🔋 Battery Museum — Know Your Power Sources!
BATTERY MUSEUM =============================================== [ 9V ] Voltage: 9.0 V Capacity: ~500 mAh | /\ | The SPRINTER: pushes hard, short distance | / \ | Used in: smoke detectors, 9V circuits +------+ Fresh = 9.0-9.6V | Tired = 7.0-8.9V [ AA ] [AA] Voltage: 1.5 V Capacity: ~2500 mAh | + || | The MARATHON RUNNER: steady, lasts long +-----++--+ Used in: remotes, toys, flashlights Fresh = 1.5-1.6V | Tired = 1.2-1.4V [AAA] Voltage: 1.5 V Capacity: ~1000 mAh | + | The JOGGER: same voltage as AA, less capacity +---+ Used in: TV remotes, small devices [CR2032] Voltage: 3.0 V Capacity: ~225 mAh ( + ) The SHORT WALK: powers watches, key fobs ( ) Fresh = 3.0-3.3V | Tired = 2.5-2.9V
Size does NOT equal voltage! The tiny 9V battery pushes 6x harder than a big AA battery.
⚡ Two Ways to Wire Batteries
📈 SERIES — Voltage Stacks!
+ to - to + to - (end to end) [AA1:1.5V]--[AA2:1.5V]--[AA3:1.5V] + - + - + - Total = 1.5 + 1.5 + 1.5 = 4.5V!
Each battery adds its voltage to the chain.
Used for: getting more push when 1 battery is not enough.
A 4-AA battery holder = 6V!
🔋 9V battery secret: 6 tiny 1.5V cells inside!
∥ PARALLEL — Capacity Doubles!
+ to + and - to - (side by side) [AA1:1.5V] --+-- (+) [AA2:1.5V] --+ 1.5V (same!) [AA3:1.5V] --+ BUT 7500 mAh (triple!)
Voltage stays the same, but capacity multiplies!
Used for: making a battery pack last much longer.
Only use identical, same-age batteries in parallel!
⚠️ Safety: never mix old + new in parallel
