📚 Key Words — Learn These!
📷 ~
Vibration
Super-fast back-and-forth shaking. Sound IS vibration! Feel your throat when you say “Ahhh” — that's your vocal cords vibrating.
🔔 ▶
Active Buzzer
A buzzer with a built-in circuit (oscillator). Just give it power and it BEEPS! Like a music box in a can.
🔊 ♬
Passive Buzzer
A buzzer that needs a changing signal to make sound. Like a speaker — it can play tunes when connected to an Arduino!
🔎 ▶
Continuity Mode
A Wand (multimeter) setting that BEEPS when a circuit path is connected. Test circuits before powering them — like checking a road on a map!

⚡ Active vs Passive Buzzers — What's the Difference?
🈮 Active Buzzer
Inside: circuit + piezo disc Touch to 9V battery: ▶▶ STEADY TONE! ▶▶ Usually has a (+) marking
Has a built-in oscillator circuit — the circuit generates the signal itself. Just supply power and it beeps without any extra help!
We use this one today.
✅ Easy — just add power!
🋼 Passive Buzzer
Inside: piezo disc only Touch to 9V battery: — just a quiet click — Needs a rapid ON/OFF signal
Just a piezo disc — like a tiny speaker. Needs a rapidly changing signal (from Arduino) to vibrate at a specific pitch. Can play actual TUNES!
We'll use this with Arduino later!
🎶 Can play music with Arduino!
🎤 Sound Science: Human hearing: 20 Hz → 20,000 Hz  |  Active buzzer: ~2,400 Hz (super annoying on purpose!)  |  Dog whistle: 23,000 Hz — dogs hear it, humans don't!  |  Hz = vibrations per second

🆕 Mission: Build a Button-Triggered Alarm with LED!
  1. 1 Hold the buzzer, touch battery terminals to it — feel the vibration in your fingertips! (+10 XP)
  2. 2 Compare Active vs Passive buzzers — touch each to 9V and listen for the difference! (+10 XP)
  3. 3 Set Wand to continuity mode (♫ symbol). Build the alarm circuit WITHOUT the battery and run all 4 continuity tests! (+40 XP)
  4. 4 Connect the battery. Press the button — BEEP! You just built a doorbell! (+30 XP)
  5. 5 Add an LED in parallel — now pressing the button triggers sound AND light at the same time!
DOORBELL CIRCUIT: 9V (+) ➔ [BUTTON] ➔ BUZZER (+) ➔ BUZZER (-) ➔ GND ↳ [330Ω] ➔ LED (+) ➔ LED (-) ➔ GND (Same button triggers BOTH buzzer AND LED 💡 🔔)
The LED helps people who cannot hear — the doorbell works for EVERYONE! 👤

🔧 Troubleshooting Quick Guide
ProblemQuick Fix
Buzzer won't beepCheck polarity — buzzer has a (+) pin!
Buzzer beeps without buttonButton wires in wrong breadboard row
LED doesn't light upFlip LED — long leg to (+) side
Wand won't beepMake sure it's set to continuity (♫) mode
Foil alarm silentTest each foil strip with Wand first!
