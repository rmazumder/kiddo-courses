## Section 4: Bonus Diagram (10 XP)

**What to look for in the drawing:**

✅ **Full Credit (10 XP) — Student should show:**
- A battery symbol (+ and − terminals marked)
- An LED with long leg connected to power side and short leg to ground
- A resistor in series with the LED (between positive and LED long leg, OR between negative and LED short leg)
- Wires connecting all parts in a complete loop
- Correct labels for each component

✅ **Partial Credit (5 XP) — Student shows:**
- Battery, LED, and resistor all present
- BUT circuit is not complete (missing wire) OR
- LED polarity is backwards OR
- Resistor is in wrong position (doesn't matter for protection)

❌ **No Credit (0 XP) — Student:**
- Is missing major components
- Does not attempt the diagram

---

## Teacher Notes & Extensions

**If a student struggles with:**

- **Polarity:** Remind them "Long leg is plus-plus-plus! Short leg goes home (ground)."
- **Resistor function:** Replay the water analogy: "Resistor = narrow pipe. Slows water/electricity down so it does not break the wheel/LED."
- **Circuit concept:** Show them a real circuit on a breadboard; break the wire and ask "What happened?"

**Fun discussion (not graded):**
- "What if you used a REALLY big resistor? The LED would be very dim!"
- "What if you used NO resistor? The LED would burn out super fast."
