# Bitcoin Explorer: The Young Investor's Adventure Course

## Welcome, Future Investor!

Have you ever heard people talking about **Bitcoin** and wondered what it is? Or seen a crazy price chart on the news and wished you could understand what all those lines mean? Well, you are about to learn exactly that!

**Bitcoin Explorer** is your adventure through the world of Bitcoin and smart investing. You will start by understanding what money and Bitcoin actually are, then learn to read price charts like a pro, spot market trends, and make smart investment decisions.

By the end of this course, you will not just know what Bitcoin is -- you will understand how markets work, how to read charts, and how to think like a smart investor.

**Important Note:** This course teaches you how markets and investing WORK. It uses Bitcoin as an example because it is one of the most well-known assets in the world. Always remember: never invest real money without talking to a parent or guardian first, and never invest more than you can afford to lose!

---

## Who Is This Course For?

- **Ages:** 10--14 years old
- **Experience needed:** None! Just curiosity and a desire to learn
- **What you will use:** Paper, pencil, and a computer or tablet for looking at charts
- **Math level:** Basic arithmetic (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division)

1. **Go in order** -- Each module builds on the previous one

## A Note for Parents and Teachers

This course teaches financial literacy and market concepts through the lens of Bitcoin and cryptocurrency. It is designed to be age-appropriate and emphasizes:

- **Education over speculation** -- We teach HOW markets work, not "how to get rich quick"
- **Risk awareness** -- Every module reinforces that investing involves risk
- **Critical thinking** -- Students learn to analyze data, not follow hype
- **Responsible habits** -- Dollar cost averaging, diversification, and patience are emphasized over "timing the market"

No real money or cryptocurrency is needed for any lesson. All activities use hypothetical scenarios and paper trading.

**Estimated completion time:** 8--12 hours (self-paced)
