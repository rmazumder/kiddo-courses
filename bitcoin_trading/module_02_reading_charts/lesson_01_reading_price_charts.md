# Lesson 1: Reading Price Charts

**Module:** 2 -- Reading Charts
**Difficulty:** Star-2 Intermediate
**Session Time:** 35--45 minutes
**Age:** 10--14 years
**XP Available:** 160 XP

---

## Your Mission Today

Welcome to the Chart Reading Academy, Explorer! Today you are going to learn one of the most important skills in trading: reading a **price chart**. Think of a price chart as a map -- but instead of showing you roads and cities, it shows you where Bitcoin's price has been over time. Treasure hunters need maps, and smart investors need charts!

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:
- Read a basic price chart (line chart)
- Understand what the X-axis (time) and Y-axis (price) mean
- Draw your own price chart from data
- Identify whether a price is going up, down, or sideways

## How to Teach This Lesson

### Step 1: Hook -- The Weather Report for Prices (5 min)

Say:
> "Have you ever watched a weather forecast on TV? They show a chart of temperatures for the week -- Monday was 70 degrees, Tuesday dropped to 60, Wednesday went up to 75. You can SEE the pattern."

> "A price chart does the EXACT same thing, but instead of temperature, it shows the PRICE of Bitcoin over time. Instead of 'Monday was 70 degrees,' it says 'Monday Bitcoin was $43,000.' Cool, right?"

Draw a simple weather chart together:

```
Temperature This Week:

  75 |         X
  70 |  X            X
  65 |     X      X
  60 |        X
     +---+---+---+---+---
      Mon Tue Wed Thu Fri
```

> "See how you can instantly tell that Wednesday was hot and Tuesday was cool? You are going to do the same thing with Bitcoin prices!"

**Award: +10 XP for connecting weather charts to price charts!**

---

### Step 2: Anatomy of a Price Chart (10 min)

**The two axes:**

```
  Price ($)
  ^
  |
  |  50,000 |  . . . . . . . . . . . . .
  |  45,000 |  . . . X . . . . . X . . .
  |  40,000 |  . X . . X . . X . . . . .
  |  35,000 |  X . . . . X X . . . . . .
  |  30,000 |  . . . . . . . . . . X . .
  |  25,000 |  . . . . . . . . . . . X .
  |         +----+---+---+---+---+---+----> Time
  |          Jan Feb Mar Apr May Jun Jul
```

**Key vocabulary:**

| Word | Kid-Friendly Definition |
|------|------------------------|
| **X-axis** | The horizontal line at the bottom -- shows TIME (days, weeks, months) |
| **Y-axis** | The vertical line on the left -- shows PRICE (in dollars) |
| **Data point** | A single dot on the chart showing the price at a specific time |
| **Line chart** | When you connect all the dots with a line -- the most basic chart type |

**Three things to look for:**

```
  UPTREND:              DOWNTREND:           SIDEWAYS:
  Price going UP        Price going DOWN      Price staying FLAT

       /                \                    -----
      /                  \                        -----
     /                    \               -----
    /                      \                   -----
```

> "When the line goes UP from left to right, the price is increasing. When it goes DOWN, the price is decreasing. When it goes SIDEWAYS, the price is not really changing much."

**Award: +20 XP for learning chart anatomy!**

---

### Step 3: Activity -- Draw Your First Price Chart (15 min)

Say:
> "Now it is YOUR turn to draw a chart! Here is 7 days of Bitcoin price data. Plot each point and connect the dots."

**The data:**

| Day | Bitcoin Price (USD) |
|-----|-------------------|
| Monday | $40,000 |
| Tuesday | $42,000 |
| Wednesday | $41,000 |
| Thursday | $43,000 |
| Friday | $45,000 |
| Saturday | $44,000 |
| Sunday | $46,000 |

**Instructions:**

1. **Draw your axes:**
   - X-axis: label it with the days (Mon through Sun)
   - Y-axis: label it with prices from $38,000 to $48,000 (go up by $2,000)

2. **Plot each data point:** Find the day on the bottom, go up to the right price, and put a dot

3. **Connect the dots** with a line using your pencil

4. **Color it:** Use GREEN for sections where the price went UP and RED for sections where it went DOWN

5. **Answer these questions:**
   - What was the highest price? (Sunday, $46,000)
   - What was the lowest price? (Monday, $40,000)
   - Is this an uptrend, downtrend, or sideways? (Uptrend -- the overall direction is up!)
   - On which day did the price drop? (Wednesday and Saturday)

> "Great job! You just read and created your first price chart. You now have a skill that millions of traders use every single day!"

**Award: +40 XP for drawing your first chart!**

---

### Step 4: Reading Real Chart Patterns (8 min)

Say:
> "Now that you can draw charts, let's learn to READ them quickly. When traders look at charts, they ask themselves one big question: 'What is the trend?'"

**The three trends:**

```
  1. UPTREND (Bullish) - Higher highs and higher lows

     Price
       |           /\
       |       /\ /  \
       |   /\ /
       |  /
       | /
       +----------------> Time
       "The price is making steps UP like a staircase going upstairs!"

  2. DOWNTREND (Bearish) - Lower highs and lower lows

     Price
       | \
       |  \
       |   \  /\
       |    \/  \  /\
       |         \/  \
       +----------------> Time
       "The price is making steps DOWN like a staircase going downstairs!"

  3. SIDEWAYS (Consolidation) - Price bouncing in a range

     Price
       |   /\    /\    /\
       |  /  \  /  \  /  \
       | /    \/    \/    \
       +----------------------> Time
       "The price is bouncing up and down like a ball in a hallway!"
```

**Ask:**
> "Look at the chart you just drew. Which pattern does it look like?"
(Uptrend! Even though there were small dips, the overall direction was up.)

> "Important lesson: prices almost NEVER go straight up or straight down. They wiggle and bounce. The trick is seeing the OVERALL direction through all the wiggles."

**Award: +20 XP for identifying chart patterns!**

---

### Step 5: Timeframes -- Zooming In and Out (5 min)

Say:
> "Here is a cool trick: the SAME Bitcoin can look completely different depending on how far you zoom in or out!"

```
  ZOOMED IN (1-hour chart):             ZOOMED OUT (1-year chart):

  Price                                  Price
    |     /\                               |                    /
    |    /  \  /\                           |               /\ /
    |   /    \/  \                          |           /\ /
    |  /          \                         |       /\ /
    | /            \                        |   /\ /
    +-----------> Time                      +-----------> Time
    "Looks scary --                         "Looks great --
     price is crashing!"                     price has been going
                                             up all year!"
```

> "This is like looking at a mountain. If you zoom in on one rock, it might look like you are going downhill. But if you zoom out and look at the whole mountain, you can see you are actually climbing UP!"

**Key takeaway:**
> "Smart investors always look at MULTIPLE timeframes. A bad day does not mean a bad year!"

**Award: +15 XP for understanding timeframes!**

---

### Step 6: Quick Quiz (5 min)

**Question 1:** "What does the Y-axis (vertical line) on a price chart show?"
(The price)
**+10 XP for a correct answer!**

**Question 2:** "If the line on a chart goes from lower-left to upper-right, what is the trend?"
(Uptrend -- the price is going up over time)
**+10 XP for a correct answer!**

**Question 3:** "Why is it important to look at different timeframes?"
(Because zooming in might show a scary dip, but zooming out shows the bigger picture. A bad day does not always mean a bad trend)
**+10 XP for a correct answer!**

**Bonus question:**
> "The chart you drew showed the price going down on Wednesday and Saturday. Does that mean Bitcoin was in a downtrend?"
(No! Even though there were dips, the overall trend for the week was UP. Small dips within an uptrend are normal)
**+25 XP bonus!**
