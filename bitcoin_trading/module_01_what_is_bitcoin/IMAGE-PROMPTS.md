## Lesson 1: What Is Money and Bitcoin?

### Filename: `lesson-01-evolution-of-money.png`

**Place in lesson:** Before Step 2: The History of Money, above the ASCII timeline.

**Prompt:**
Bright, colorful flat-design illustration for kids ages 10-14. Friendly cartoon style, no dark or scary elements. A horizontal timeline showing the evolution of money from left to right with five stages: (1) seashells and beads labeled "5000 BC - Barter & Shells" with a cartoon cave person trading, (2) gold bars and nuggets labeled "3000 BC - Precious Metals" with a shiny gold glow, (3) stamped metal coins labeled "600 BC - Coins" with a king's face on them, (4) paper bills labeled "1661 AD - Paper Money" with a bank building behind them, (5) a glowing golden Bitcoin symbol labeled "2009 - Bitcoin" with a laptop showing the Bitcoin logo. A cheerful cartoon kid walks along the timeline pointing at each stage with wonder. Each stage is connected by golden arrows. The background transitions from earthy brown (ancient) to bright blue (modern). Labels are short and directly on the image.

## Lesson 3: What Is Trading?

### Filename: `lesson-03-supply-and-demand.png`

**Place in lesson:** Before Step 2: Supply and Demand, above the ice cream truck analogy.

**Prompt:**
Bright, colorful flat-design illustration for kids ages 10-14. Friendly cartoon style, no dark or scary elements. A two-panel comparison using the ice cream truck analogy. Top panel labeled "High Demand + Low Supply = Price UP" shows: one ice cream truck surrounded by a huge crowd of 20+ thirsty cartoon kids on a sunny hot day, with a price sign showing "$10" and a green upward arrow. The truck driver looks overwhelmed but happy. Bottom panel labeled "Low Demand + High Supply = Price DOWN" shows: five ice cream trucks lined up on the same street but only 3 kids walking by looking uninterested, with price signs showing "$1" and red downward arrows. The truck drivers look bored. Between the panels, a golden Bitcoin coin with arrows showing "Same rules apply to Bitcoin!" The sun blazes in the top panel (hot = more demand). Color coding: green for price up, red for price down, gold for Bitcoin.
