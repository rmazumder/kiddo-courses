**Difficulty:** Star-1 Beginner

**Recommended Age:** 10--14 years
**Prerequisites:** None -- this is the very beginning!
**Total XP Available:** 460 XP

---

## What This Module Is About

Before you can understand trading and investing, you need to know what **money** is, why **Bitcoin** was invented, and what it means to **trade** something. Think of this module as your passport -- you need it before you can travel into the world of markets and charts!

In this module, you will discover how money evolved from seashells to digital coins, learn how Bitcoin works under the hood (without needing to be a computer genius), and understand the basics of buying and selling in a market.

## Module Goals

By the end of this module, you will:

- Understand what money is and why Bitcoin was created
- Know how Bitcoin transactions work (in simple terms)
- Understand the difference between a currency and an asset
- Know what buying, selling, and trading mean
- Be ready to learn how to read price charts in Module 2!

## Parent/Instructor Notes

- Sessions of **30--45 minutes** work best for each lesson
- **Lesson 1** focuses on the concept of money -- encourage kids to think about what makes something valuable
- **Lesson 2** simplifies blockchain technology using physical games -- no coding required
- **Lesson 3** uses trading card games as an analogy that most kids already understand
- **Track XP visually** -- use a poster, whiteboard, or notebook. The gamification only works if kids can see their progress!
- Ages 10--11: Focus on the analogies and hands-on activities
- Ages 12--14: Encourage deeper discussion about why Bitcoin was invented and how it differs from regular money
