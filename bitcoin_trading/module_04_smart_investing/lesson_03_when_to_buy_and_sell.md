### Step 2: The Decision Framework (10 min)

Say:
> "Before you buy or sell, run through this checklist. It is like a pilot's pre-flight checklist -- you go through it EVERY time."

**The Buy/Sell/Wait Checklist:**

```
  THE INVESTOR'S CHECKLIST

  Before ANY decision, answer these questions:

  1. TREND: What is the overall trend?
     [ ] Uptrend (bullish) -> Favors buying
     [ ] Downtrend (bearish) -> Favors waiting or selling
     [ ] Sideways -> Favors waiting

  2. SUPPORT/RESISTANCE: Where are the key levels?
     [ ] Near support? -> Could be a good buy zone
     [ ] Near resistance? -> Could be a sell zone
     [ ] In the middle? -> No clear signal

  3. MOVING AVERAGE: Where is price vs. the MA?
     [ ] Above MA -> Bullish signal
     [ ] Below MA -> Bearish signal

  4. RISK: Does this fit my risk plan?
     [ ] Am I only using money I can afford to lose?
     [ ] Have I set a stop loss?
     [ ] Is my portfolio diversified?

  5. EMOTIONS: Am I calm and rational?
     [ ] Am I buying because of FOMO? (BAD)
     [ ] Am I selling because of panic? (BAD)
     [ ] Am I following my plan? (GOOD)
```

**Three possible decisions:**

| Decision | When to Use It |
|----------|---------------|
| **BUY** | Uptrend + near support + above MA + fits risk plan |
| **SELL** | Downtrend + near resistance + below MA + stop loss hit |
| **WAIT** | Mixed signals, sideways market, unsure, emotional |

> "Here is a secret that most beginners do not know: the best decision is often to WAIT. Not doing anything IS a valid strategy! Patience is an investor's superpower."

**Award: +25 XP for learning the decision framework!**

### Step 3: Activity -- The Grand Trading Simulation (20 min)

Say:
> "This is it -- your final challenge! You are going to paper trade through 12 months of simulated Bitcoin price data. You start with $120 and need to make the best decisions you can."

**Rules:**
- You start with $120 in cash and 0 BTC
- Each month, you must choose: BUY (spend $10-$30), SELL (sell some/all BTC), or WAIT (do nothing)
- Use your checklist for EVERY decision
- Track everything in your trading log

**The data:**

| Month | Price | Trend | Near Support? | Near Resistance? | What You See |
|-------|-------|-------|--------------|-----------------|-------------|
| 1 | $40,000 | Starting | Near support ($38K) | Resistance at $50K | Just starting -- observe! |
| 2 | $42,000 | Slight up | No | No | Small green candles |
| 3 | $45,000 | Uptrend forming | No | No | Higher highs, higher lows |
| 4 | $48,000 | Uptrend | No | Approaching resistance | Getting close to ceiling |
| 5 | $50,000 | Uptrend | No | AT resistance ($50K) | Price touching the ceiling! |
| 6 | $47,000 | Bounced down | No | Rejected from $50K | Red candles appearing |
| 7 | $43,000 | Turning down | No | No | Trend line broke! |
| 8 | $38,000 | Downtrend | AT support ($38K) | No | Price at the floor |
| 9 | $35,000 | Downtrend | Support broke! | No | Falling below old floor |
| 10 | $32,000 | Downtrend | New support forming? | No | Price stabilizing? |
| 11 | $35,000 | Sideways | Testing $32K as support | $38K now resistance | Small green candles |
| 12 | $40,000 | Recovering | $35K holding as support | $42K resistance | Uptrend forming? |

**Your Trading Log:**

```
  +-------+---------+----------+----------+-------+--------+----------+
  | Month | Price   | Decision | Amount   | BTC   | Cash   | Total    |
  |       |         |          | Spent    | Owned | Left   | Value    |
  +-------+---------+----------+----------+-------+--------+----------+
  | 1     | $40,000 |          |          |       | $120   |          |
  | 2     | $42,000 |          |          |       |        |          |
  | 3     | $45,000 |          |          |       |        |          |
  | ...   |         |          |          |       |        |          |
  | 12    | $40,000 |          |          |       |        |          |
  +-------+---------+----------+----------+-------+--------+----------+
```

**Walk through the first few months together:**

**Month 1 ($40,000):** "Price is near support at $38,000. The trend is unclear (just starting). What should you do?"
- Suggested: BUY a small amount ($10). Near support is a reasonable place to start. Or WAIT for a clearer signal. Both are valid!

**Month 5 ($50,000):** "Price has hit resistance at $50,000. The trend has been up for months. What should you do?"
- Suggested: SELL some (take profits) or WAIT. Buying at resistance is risky because the price often bounces down from here.

**Month 8 ($38,000):** "Price has dropped to support at $38,000. There are red candles. What do you do?"
- This is tricky! It COULD bounce up from support (buy opportunity) or break through support (more losses). A small buy with a stop loss below $36,000 could work. Or wait for confirmation.

**Month 10 ($32,000):** "Everything is down. Bear market. Are you panicking?"
- If you followed DCA, you are still buying! Low prices = more BTC per dollar.

**Let them complete months 2--12 on their own, then discuss their results.**

**Discussion questions:**
> "What was your best decision? Your worst?"
> "Did you ever feel like panicking? What did you do?"
> "If you used DCA ($10/month), what would your result be?"

**DCA benchmark:** $10/month x 12 = $120 invested
- DCA total BTC: roughly 0.00315 BTC
- DCA value at Month 12 ($40,000): approximately $126
- DCA result: small profit with zero stress!
