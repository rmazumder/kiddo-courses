## What This Module Is About

This is it -- the final module, and the MOST important one. You have learned about Bitcoin, charts, and trends. Now you are going to learn something that separates smart investors from reckless gamblers: **how to make wise decisions with money**.

In this module, you will learn about risk management (never lose more than you can afford), dollar cost averaging (a strategy so simple it is almost unfair), and how to think about when to buy and sell. This is where everything you have learned comes together!

**Remember:** This course teaches you HOW investing works. Always talk to a parent or guardian before investing real money, and never invest more than you can afford to lose.

## Parent/Instructor Notes

- This is the most IMPORTANT module from a financial literacy perspective
- **Risk management** is the core message -- reinforce that protecting your money comes FIRST
- **Dollar cost averaging** is presented as the simplest, most kid-friendly strategy because it removes emotion from investing
- The **trading simulation** in Lesson 3 ties the whole course together
- Emphasize throughout: this is educational. No child should be investing real money without parental guidance
- Ages 10--11: Focus on the concepts of saving, patience, and not putting all eggs in one basket
- Ages 12--14: Encourage them to think critically about investment ads and social media hype
- Consider having a conversation about real-world investing after completing this module
