## What This Module Is About

You can read charts and spot support and resistance levels. Now it is time to learn how to identify the BIG PICTURE: **market trends**. In this module, you will learn about bull and bear markets (named after real animals!), draw trend lines on charts, and discover a powerful smoothing tool called moving averages.

By the end of this module, you will be able to look at any price chart and say with confidence: "The trend is UP," "The trend is DOWN," or "There is no clear trend right now."

## Module Goals

By the end of this module, you will:

- Know what bull and bear markets are and how to tell them apart
- Draw accurate trend lines on a chart
- Calculate and plot a moving average
- Understand how trends help you make better decisions
- Be ready to learn smart investing strategies in Module 4!

## Parent/Instructor Notes

- This module builds directly on Module 2 -- make sure the learner is comfortable with charts first
- **Moving averages** (Lesson 3) involve some math (calculating averages). For ages 10--11, consider doing the math together. For ages 12--14, let them try independently
- The bull/bear analogy is fun and memorable -- consider having the learner draw or look up pictures of bulls and bears
- Encourage them to look at real Bitcoin charts and try to identify trends. TradingView.com offers free charts with moving average overlays
- This is the most analytical module -- celebrate effort even if the concepts take time to click
