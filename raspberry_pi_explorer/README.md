## вҡ пёҸ Safety Rules вҖ” SUPER IMPORTANT! вҡ пёҸ

Before we start, every great explorer follows safety rules. These keep you AND
your Pi safe!

### рҹ”ҙ Rule 1: ALWAYS Turn Off Your Pi Before Wiring!
```
вҡЎ DANGER ZONE вҡЎ
Never connect or disconnect wires while your Pi is powered on!
This can damage your Pi or your components.
Always: Shut down вҶ’ Unplug power вҶ’ THEN change wires
```

### рҹ”ҙ Rule 2: Double-Check Your Wires!
```
Before powering on, ALWAYS check:
вң… Positive (+) goes to positive
вң… Negative (-) goes to negative (Ground/GND)
вң… Resistors are in place for LEDs
вң… No loose wires touching each other
```

### рҹ”ҙ Rule 3: Voltage Matters!
```
рҹҚ“ Raspberry Pi GPIO pins use 3.3V вҖ” that's TINY and SAFE
вҡ пёҸ  The 5V pins can DAMAGE sensors that expect 3.3V
вқҢ  NEVER connect GPIO pins directly to 5V!
вқҢ  NEVER connect two GPIO pins directly to each other!
```

### рҹ”ҙ Rule 4: Ask for Help!
```
рҹ‘ЁвҖҚрҹ‘©вҖҚрҹ‘§вҖҚрҹ‘Ұ Always have a parent or adult nearby when:
   - Plugging in the power supply
   - You smell something burning (unplug immediately!)
   - Something doesn't look right
   - You're not sure about a wire connection
```

### рҹ”ҙ Rule 5: Treat Your Pi With Care!
```
рҹ§Ө Handle your Pi by the edges (avoid touching the chips)
рҹ’§ Keep liquids FAR away from your Pi
рҹ§І Keep magnets away from your Pi and SD card
рҹ“Ұ Store in a safe, dry place when not in use
```
