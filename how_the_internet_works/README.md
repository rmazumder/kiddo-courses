## 📚 What You'll Learn

By the time you complete this course, you'll be able to:

- ✅ Explain the difference between the **Internet** and the **World Wide Web**
- ✅ Understand how data travels across the world in **milliseconds**
- ✅ Know what **IP addresses** and **DNS** are (and why they matter)
- ✅ Describe the **client-server model** — what happens when you visit a website
- ✅ Understand **HTTP, HTTPS**, and why that little 🔒 padlock matters
- ✅ Use real **terminal commands** like `ping`, `nslookup`, and `traceroute`
- ✅ Know how to **stay safe online** — passwords, phishing, encryption
- ✅ Complete an epic **Internet Detective** final project! 🕵️
