# Python Quest: A Programming Adventure for Young Coders!

## Welcome, Future Programmer!

You are about to embark on an incredible journey into the world of **Python programming**!
By the end of this course, you will be able to write your own programs, solve puzzles
with code, and even **build your own text-based adventure game**!

No experience needed -- just curiosity and a willingness to try!

---

## Who Is This Course For?

- Ages **8 to 14**
- Complete beginners (no coding experience required!)
- Anyone who loves games, puzzles, animals, or adventures

## How Points and Badges Work

### XP (Experience Points)
- Reading a lesson: **10 XP**
- Completing an activity: **15 XP**
- Finishing a quiz: **20 XP**
- Completing a module challenge: **30 XP**
- Finishing the capstone game: **100 XP**

### Badges
You earn a badge after completing each module. Collect all 7 to become a
**Python Quest Champion**!

### Bonus Stars
Look for the BONUS STAR challenges in lessons. These are optional extra-hard
problems for adventurers who want an extra challenge!

---

## How to Use This Course

1. **Go in order** -- each module builds on the one before it.
2. **Read the README.md** in each lesson folder first.
3. **Run the example code** (the `.py` files) to see what happens.
4. **Try the exercises** -- fill in the blanks and run them!
5. **Take the quiz** at the end of each module.
6. **Complete the challenge** to earn your badge!
7. **Track your progress** in `progress_tracker.py`.
